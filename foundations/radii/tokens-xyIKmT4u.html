Radii

In 2022 the Tripadvisor Design System began moving towards rounding corners for images, containers, and many other assets. Our Radii system is made up of three radii sizes: Small, Medium, and Large; as well as fully rounded. A larger asset uses a larger radii.

 

Why we round corners

Research shows that rounded corners are easier for our eyes to process and can relieve users’ cognitive load for the following reasons:

A container with sharp corners points outward, forcing us to focus on what’s outside of the container. On the other hand, rounded containers point inwards towards the center of the container, shifting our focus to the contents inside the container.

Our brains can scan a container with a smooth rounded edge more easily, while sharp corners can cause our brains to pause and will interrupt thought.

When two containers are next to each other, sharp containers make it hard to tell which of the two sides belong to which container, this is because each rectangle side is exactly a straight line. The sides of a rounded rectangle are unique because the lines curve towards the rectangle it belongs to. In this way, we are able to process space or pathways more easily between rounded containers instead of sharp container.

 

 

 

Sizes

 

X-Large Radius (12px)

The x-large radius is for x-large sized assets. X-Large only used for Desktop and large modals  Cards or components that have full-width images (excluding full-bleed cards like the full width feature cards & inset image feature cards).

Examples: Full width hero images, Full width gallery components, full width containers, large stylized cards.

radius-xlarge: 12px

 

 

Large Radius (8px)

The large radius is for larger sized assets.   Cards or components that have full-width images (excluding full-bleed cards like the full width feature cards & inset image feature cards).

Examples: Vertical Cards, Stylized Prompt Cards, Image background cards.

radius-large: 8px

 

 

Medium Radius (6px)

The medium radius is for medium sized assets.   Cards or components that have images that do not take up the full-width of the card but are horizontally aligned next to other content.

Examples: Horizontal Cards, List Items, Booking Cards, Typeahead Images (including icon containers), images inside of Reviews.

radius-medium: 6px

 

 

Small Radius (4px)

Use for non-interactive components (not images) like Merchandising Labels, Status Labels, and Tags.

Examples: Horizontal Cards, List Items, Booking Cards, Typeahead Images (including icon containers), images inside of Reviews.

radius-small: 4px

 

 

Full Rounded Radius (1000px)

Interactive components like Chips and Buttons.


Examples: Horizontal Cards, List Items, Booking Cards, Typeahead Images (including icon containers), images inside of Reviews.

radius-full-rounded: 1000px
