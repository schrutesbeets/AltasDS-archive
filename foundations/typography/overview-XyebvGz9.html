Typography

Typography tokens are the foundational building blocks that store and manage all typographic styles and properties, ensuring consistency and scalability across a design system.

  • Overview
  • Tokens

 

 

 

General concepts

Our new typography system is flexible, intentional, and adaptable, providing a future-proof foundation for seamless scaling and consistent usage across our products. This is achieved through:

Flexibility: The Major Second scale with a root of 12 and a numerical naming convention offers a logical foundation for seamless size adjustments and evolution as design needs change.

Intentionality: A comprehensive audit of type usage informed the definition of purposeful roles and names for each size, enabling designers to easily select the right size and ensuring product-wide consistency.

Adaptability: Theming predefines type behavior across viewports, ensuring consistent scaling and supporting language-specific adjustments and future accessibility improvements without extra design intervention.

 

 

 

Usage

There are two families of type styles:

Adaptive

These styles have specific sizes based on the device to ensure optimal readability and visual hierarchy

↳ Large (only for desktop and tablet use cases)

↳ Small (only for mobile phone use cases)

Fixed

These styles remain consistent across all devices

Important Note: The Large and Small variants of Adaptive styles are not optional; they must be used for their corresponding device types.

 

 

 

Intentional Roles

Each role now has a defined use case and clear guidelines for designers on how, when, and why to use each size. The system is fluid, allowing creative freedom in mixing and matching type, while ensuring it remains simple, manageable, and purposeful.
