TA Logo

The Tripadvisor Logo is the official primary brand mark used to represent the Tripadvisor brand consistently across digital and print applications.

  • Overview
  • Dos & Don'ts

Best practices

 

Color

Do

Keep the pre-defined color of the TA logos.

Don't

Do not alter the color of the TA logos.

 

Size

Do

The TA logo can be resized as long as proportions are maintained.

Don't

Do not resize the logo to dimensions that are inaccessible or disproportionately large.
