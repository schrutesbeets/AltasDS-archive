AI-powered cross-selling

These AI-powered features help users discover new ways to expand their trips. These modules help us introduce relevant products based on a user’s previous actions or expressed intent. 

Please note: this section does not cover all AI products within Tripadvisor. For more detailed descriptions of our AI Travel Assistant (Ollie), Review summaries, AI trip builder, and the general company overview of AI, please review the AI at Tripadvisor | Universal Messaging Guide.

Explicit Intent

Asks users whether they’ve booked a hotel, and shepherds them to create a trip, compare pricing, or read reviews based on their response. Currently live on /HR pages.

GAI Anchor Points 

Invites users to use AI to generate personalized recommendations for things to do based on a specific location or attraction. Currently live on AR pages; in production on hotel list pages.
