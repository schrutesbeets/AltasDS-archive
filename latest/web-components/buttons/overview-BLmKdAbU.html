Buttons

Buttons trigger an action such as submitting a form or showing/hiding an interface component.

Anatomy & Size

 

Button Anatomy

Buttons consist of an optional, interchangeable icon, and/or label text within a container. When in a loading state, they contain a spinner icon. Brand and Secondary buttons all include an outline.

 

Button Size

Buttons are supported in a range of heights: 40px, 48px and 56px. The Brand button is the only button supported at a 32px height with a container that hugs it content. Button widths are variable. They can either hug their contents, or stretch to fill a larger container.

 

Borderless Button Anatomy

Borderless Buttons consist of underlined label text within a transparent container with vertical padding. Optionally, they may include an interchangeable icon, a chevron down icon for dropdown contexts, or an external link icon for outbound link contexts.

 

Borderless Button Size

Borderless Buttons are supported in a range of heights: 24px, 32px, 40px and 48px. Borderless Button widths are variable based on their length of text.

 

Circular Button Anatomy

Circular Buttons can consist of an interchangeable icon, label text, or the combination of the two, within a rounded container. The clear variant has a transparent container.

 

Circular Button Size

Circular Buttons are supported in a range of heights: 40px, 48px, and 56px. Circular buttons that contain a single icon are fully rounded. Those containing text are more pill shaped.

 

Play Button Size

Circular Buttons are supported in a range of heights: 48px, 56px, and 64px.

 

 

Behavior

All button types are supported in a range of standard interaction states

 

Button and Interaction States

 

Left to right, top to bottom: brand default, brand hover, brand pressed, brand disabled, primary default, primary hover, primary pressed, primary disabled, secondary default, secondary hover, secondary pressed, secondary disabled, Ollie AI default, Ollie AI hover, Ollie AI pressed, Ollie AI disabled

 

Borderless Button Interaction State

Left to right: default, hover, pressed, disabled

 

Circular Button Interaction States

Left to right, top to bottom: primary default, primary hover, primary pressed, primary disabled, secondary default, secondary hover, secondary pressed, secondary disabled, clear default, clear hover, clear pressed, clear disabled

 

Play Button Interaction States

Left to right, top to bottom: play enabled, play hover, play pressed, pause enabled, pause hover, pause pressed
