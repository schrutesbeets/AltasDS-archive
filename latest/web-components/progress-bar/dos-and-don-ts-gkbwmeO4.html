Also known as Progress Tracker or Progress Meter

  • Dos & Don'ts

Best practices

 

Color

Do

Use default tokens for completed steps and the Progress Bar track so users can understand their progress at a glance.

Don't

Do not change color tokens that have been applied to the Progress Bar. These tokens have been chosen specifically to adhere to accessibility requirements.

 

Progression Length

Caution

Even though capable, a Progress Bar is unnecessary for a two-step process.

Don't

Do not use for a process with more than 9-10 steps since it can become overwhelming to our travelers. In such cases, consider breaking the process into larger, more manageable sections.
