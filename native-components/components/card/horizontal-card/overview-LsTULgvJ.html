Horizontal Card

Horizontal cards are used to display summarized content or POIs in a compact, side-by-side layout, making them ideal for areas where vertical space is limited.

Anatomy

If any of the prebuilt horizontal cards do not fit your use case, you are welcome to design custom cards and submit them for intake. Be mindful of ordering rules defined below:

 

Order

For horizontal cards, the image always leads on the left followed by the fixed tier and then an action. The layer order in the fixed tier can not be moved around, but can be turned on or off.

 

Media/Icon

The media container or an icon frame are the only types of assets supported in horizontal cards. Imagery also cannot support any badging or icon action.

Fixed tier

Layers within the fixed tier can be turned on and off, but their order is fixed. However, a card title is required.

Icon button

An icon button is the trailing action element in horizontal cards. It supports quick, single-tap interactions such as saving, liking, or closing. Only one icon button is supported per card.

 

 

 

States & Appearance

 

Interactive states

Horizontal cards use state layers for interaction states. The available states are enabled, pressed, and focused. The icon buttons within the cards have their own interactive states as well.
