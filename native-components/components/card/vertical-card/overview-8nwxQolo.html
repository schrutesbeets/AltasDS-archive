Anatomy

If any of the prebuilt verticals cards do not fit your use case, you are welcome to design custom cards and submit them for intake. Be mindful of ordering rules defined below:

 

Order

For vertical cards, the image always comes first followed by the fixed tier and then optional custom slots. The layer order in the fixed tier can not be moved around, but can be turned on or off.

 

Fixed tier

Layers within the fixed tier can be turned on and off, but their order is fixed. However, a card title is required.

Custom elements

Vertical cards can include a few optional elements, with support for up to two custom slots. The examples above highlight the three most commonly used elements. If your design requires an element outside of the top three, please submit an intake form for review and consideration.
