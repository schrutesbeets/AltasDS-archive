Date Picker

The date picker allows users to select a date or range of dates depending on the context in which it appears.

Variants

There are three variants of date picker: Default, Heat map, and Price map.

Anatomy & Size

 

Default

The default date picker allows users to select or edit the date manually. The Guest/Room input field is optional, if applicable, and provides quick access to adjust guest and room counts. An optional chip row surfaces shortcut options for common ranges. The calendar is required and displays the month view for browsing and selecting dates.

 

Heat map

The heat map variant displays dates with color-coded indicators that reflect pricing trends. The heat map colored dates are required to help users quickly compare whether a date is cheaper, average, or higher in cost. The legend is required to explain the meaning of each color category to support easy interpretation.

 

Price map

The price map variant shows each date alongside a required price label, making nightly rates visible at a glance. A price footnote is required to provide context about the currency and can include any additional notes related to pricing.

 

 

 

States & Appearance

 

Calendar date states

Calendar dates have a range of states to show contextual information, range, and availability.

 

Calendar date interactive states

Calendar dates use state layers for interaction states. The available states are enabled, pressed, focused, selected, and disabled.
