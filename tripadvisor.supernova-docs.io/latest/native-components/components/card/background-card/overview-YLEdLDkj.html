Background Card

Background cards are used to display information geared towards discovery and inspiration. They should focus on sparking curiosity and guiding users to explore further.

Anatomy & Size

 

Anatomy

Background cards have an image as the entire background. It contains a required image, image scrim, and card title. Optional elements include badge, eyebrow and supporting content copy, and lastly a button.

 

Size

Background cards can only utilize an image with a 3:4 or 1:1 aspect ratio.

 

 

 

States & Appearance

 

Interactive states (Whole card)

Background cards use state layers for interaction states. The available states are enabled, pressed, and focused.

 

Interactive states (Explicit tap target)

For background cards with an explicit tap target, interaction states apply only to the button.

 

Dark mode

The content within background cards remain fixed when in dark mode.
