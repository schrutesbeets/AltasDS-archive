Card

Contained units of information and actions related to a single subject.

 

 

 

Guidelines

 

Cards are frequently used in many cases across our platform. They help to visually group content and information, and often serve as an entry point for users to learn more details about a place or subject.

 

 

 

Types

There are four types of cards: Vertical, Horizontal, Background, and Message cards.

 

 

 

 

Which card should I use?

 

Background card

Background cards are used to display information geared towards discovery and inspiration.

 

Horizontal card

Horizontal cards are used to display summarized content or POIs in a compact, side-by-side layout.

 

Message card

Message cards are reserved to message campaign content.

 

Vertical card

Vertical cards are used to display a summarized collection of news, content, or a POI.
