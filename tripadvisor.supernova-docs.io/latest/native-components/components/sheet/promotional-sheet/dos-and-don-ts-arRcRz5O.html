Promotional Sheet

Promotional bottom sheets highlight marketing content, special offers, or calls to action intended to drive user engagement or conversion by driving awareness while keeping the experience lightweight.

  • Overview
  • Dos & Don'ts

Properties

 

Image

Promotional bottoms sheets have optional images (16x9) within the content container.

 

Header & Subcopy

A header is optional and can introduce or summarize the promotion in a clear, engaging way. Positioned within the content container, it should be short, action-oriented, and communicate the core message at a glance. Subcopy is required to ensure the promotion remains clear and easy to understand.

 

Value props

Value props are optional and can be used to highlight key benefits or features of the promotion. They should be concise, scannable, and help reinforce the core message without adding unnecessary complexity.

 

CTAs

Promotional sheets must include a primary CTA that clearly communicates the intended action. It should be prominent, easy to understand, and drive users toward the main goal of the promotion.

 

 

 

Activation

Most promotional bottom sheets are auto-triggered when landing on a specific screen.

 

Tertiary buttons & Text links

Low-emphasis calls to action like tertiary buttons or underlined text links that invite users to explore more information. When tapped, they can open a promotional bottom sheet with upselling information and a primary CTA.

 

 

 

Behavior

 

Dismissal

Promotional bottom sheets can be dismissed by:

  • Tapping or clicking the dismiss/close icon
  • Tapping or clocking on the scrim
  • Swiping or dragging the bottom sheet down
  • Tapping or clicking on the CTA

 

Text wrapping

It’s recommended that the header of a promotion sheet take up at most two lines of text (~30 to 60-65 characters). While the heaeder will not truncate, it is best to keep it short and to the point.
The sheet content (sub copy) does not have a copy limit, but it’s best to keep copy to the point.

 

 

 

Best practices

 

Imagery

Do

Images should not contain text or content messaging within it. Any visual elements should be exported within the image.

Don't

Do not include text within the bottom sheet image.
