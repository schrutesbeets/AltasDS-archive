Sign-in modules

Prompts users to sign in to see account details or complete an action. Meant to be brief and action-oriented to keep users encouraged and engaged. 

Feature-specific screens 

Used when products, like Trips, require a user sign-in to display features like itineraries, saves, and more. 

Tone leans into the general brand tone. Friendly, familiar, and instructional, but requires product-specific language to help encourage account creation and sign-in with the proper context.

Action-driven sheets 

Full-screen or bottom-sheet takeovers are triggered by specific actions, such as leaving a review, sharing a tip, or saving an item. Should be short and specific to the action, when possible. 

Tone is functional, simple. Most will follow the same rhythm of Sign in to [complete the necessary action], unless placement in a specific flow dictates otherwise (see general and itineraries bottom sheets).
