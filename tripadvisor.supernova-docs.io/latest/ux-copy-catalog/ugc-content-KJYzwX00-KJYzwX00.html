UGC Content

Content removal

Guides users as they remove their own content from the site.

Length: Keep each “reason” as short as possible. Can be short clauses instead of full sentences.

Tone: Clear, plainspoken. 

Verbiage: Repurpose existing language when applicable, but phrasing can change based on need.

Feedback modules

Modules used to gauge and collect user feedback/sentiment on traveler-submitted travel guidance or AI-generated responses.

Length: Keep each “reason” as short as possible. Can be short clauses instead of full sentences.

Tone: Clear, plainspoken. 

Verbiage: Repurpose existing language when possible, but exact phrasing can change depending on application, context, and space constraints. Just be sure to maintain the same sentiment and brevity.
