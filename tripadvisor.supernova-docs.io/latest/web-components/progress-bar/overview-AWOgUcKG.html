Progress Bar

A representation of a userâ€™s progress through a series of discrete steps.

Also known as Progress Tracker or Progress Meter

  • Overview
  • Dos & Don'ts

 

 

Anatomy & Size

 

Anatomy

Progress Bar consists of a progress indicator and the track it sits within. The progress indicator grows with the total number of increments the bar displays.

 

Size

Progress Bars have a fixed height of 4px, and have a variable width.

 

 

 

Behavior

Each of the 9 variants can be incremented to less than or equal to the total number of steps. For example, a Progress Bar with 10 steps can be incremented to any step from 1-10.

 

States

Top to bottom, left to right: increments 1 through 10, of a 10 step Progress Bar
