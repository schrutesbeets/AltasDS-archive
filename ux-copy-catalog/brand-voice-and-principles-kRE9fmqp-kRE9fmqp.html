Brand voice & principles

Voice

The Tripadvisor voice is conversational & uncomplicated. 
Our voice is how our brand personality comes to life. As The Candid Companion, our voice is conversational and uncomplicated, just the right casual combination to keep Passionate Travelers feeling welcomed, comfortable, motivated, and clear about where to go and what to do.

Brand Personality: The Candid Companion

At Tripadvisor, no one takes a trip alone. Together with our community, we support travelers and steer them through every stage of planning, booking, and sharing their trips. As the Candid Companion, Tripadvisor is equal parts friendly resource and expert guide, all in service of making everyone a better traveler. We provide the best trip advice, which comes from real travelers’ real experiences. We’re the greatest kind of companion out there: an honest one.

Audience: The Passionate Traveler

Passionate Travelers are excited about travel and love to plan—everything from weekend trips with pals to summer retreats with the family. And when Passionate Travelers aren’t on vacation? They’re thinking about what’s next: Trips that are well researched, seriously enjoyed, and gracefully shared. They’re intentional and value every trip they take.

Brand Principles

Keep it simple
Making things easy for travelers is core to who we are  as a brand and business, and that includes how we speak. We want to set travelers up for their best trip yet, not weigh them down with extra information or language.


Warm it up
A good travel companion is personable, someone who feels like they could become a friend. That comes from endearing, engaging communication that feels personal but not overly intimate (think: a fun acquaintance, not a lifetime BFF). If what you’re writing seems approachable to you, you’re on the  right track.


Find the fun
We’re passionate about travel, just like our audience, so we want to bring that enthusiasm into how we speak. Every trip  is different. Every traveler has their ups and downs. We’re here to celebrate all of it with good humor and good energy.


Write with insight
We’ve been in business for 25 years and know a thing or two about taking a trip and making it great. We use our expertise to validate and help guide travelers, as well as create and curate the best trip guidance around.

Strategic DS Values for UX Copy

Traveler first  
Standardized copy helps ensure that we always meet the mark on consistency.


A tool for scale 
Standardized copy helps teams iterate, learn, and make improvements. Keep language as relevant, timely, and contextual as possible, but prioritize personalization (trip names, user names, etc.) wherever possible. 


Made effortless 
Standardized copy allows us to quickly envision, build, and scale connected experiences that delight our travelers, all while asking as little of them as possible.


Keep it real 
UX copy should support the Tripadvisor brand’s goal of fostering trust and authenticity. Brevity is a gift, and efficiency is kindness. 


Welcome all travelers 
Prioritize copy solutions that are welcoming and accessible to all.
