Disclaimers & legal

Short text clauses that outline some of the high-level details of legal terms. Most often used for offers and promotions, as well as in checkout/transaction areas. 

How to write a disclaimer

Length: Variable, but always prioritize clarity and simplicity of language over brevity.

Tone: Clear, plainspoken, leaning toward formality. 

Date formats: Full date (e.g., September 12, 2025) to avoid confusion. Can shorten months (e.g., Sept, Oct) when necessary for space. Only use number format (09-12-25) when really short on space.

In-line text links: Often used to link to a full set of terms and conditions. Usually comes at the end of a clause and does not require end punctuation unless at the end of a full sentence.
