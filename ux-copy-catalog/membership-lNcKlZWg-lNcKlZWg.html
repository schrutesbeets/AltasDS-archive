Membership

Badges and achievements

Overview
Recognizes Tripadvisor users for their contributions to the community—photos, reviews, and overall views on content.  

Where it lives
Featured under the “Account info” section of a user’s profile, but can be directly accessed through www.tripadvisor.com/MyAchievements.

Other use cases
As membership becomes a greater part of Tripadvisor’s offering to users, we may find more ways to use the language in other places—to encourage reviews, to make public trips, etc. 

Tripadvisor Rewards

Tripadvisor’s free rewards program makes it easier for members to discover, plan, and book their trip in one place and get rewarded at every step of the way. Once travelers sign up, they automatically get access to the full Tripadvisor experience, including the best trip planning app, the ability to book trips, rewards, achievements, and community contributions. Members can access benefits on the web, but the majority of benefits are only in the app.


Key pillars include:

  • 5% back in Trip Cash on stays and experiences booked in the app
  • Earn bonus Trip Cash for planning and sharing content
  • Price match guarantee—only when booking in the app
For full details on Tripadvisor Rewards and the other pillars of membership, review the full Consumer Mktg | Membership | Positioning + Messaging Guide.
