Words to watch out for

Watch these words

Use recommendations, not recs.
Lead with recommendations in product copy to ensure clarity for users. Can use an alternative, like picks, when tight on space. 


Use features or products, not tools.
Never refer to our offerings as a “tool” in user-facing copy. Refer to individual products and features to elevate our suite of offerings. 


Use hotels before using “stays.”
Mention the word “hotels” in your product copy or headline first.  Use “stays” as a secondary term (as a way to diversify language, as necessary). Stay is fine in body copy (as in “places to stay”).

Words to use sparingly

Anything on this list is technically OK to use, but try to find better, more specific words/phrases whenever possible.

  • across the pond
  • beautiful
  • big
  • bucket list
  • City nicknames:
    • Big Apple
    • The Big Easy
    • City of Lights
    • City that never sleeps
    • Sin City
    • [xy honorific] City
    • City of Brotherly Love
  • delicious
  • fun
  • great outdoors

House spellings

  • A.D.  |  B.C.
  • aka (abbreviation for “also known as”)
  • best seller (noun) | best-selling (adj)
  • Bachelor’s degree | Bachelor of Arts
  • café
  • cancellation | canceled | cancel
  • check in (verb)  |  check-in (noun)
  • do’s and don’ts
  • email (not e-mail)
  • everyday (adj) | every day (noun)
  • every time
  • firepit
  • login (noun)  |  log in (verb)  |  log in to (not log into)  |  log-in (adj)
  • Master’s degree | Master of Arts
  • mid-Atlantic
  • northern lights
  • Northerner
  • okay
  • Pacific Northwest
  • São Paulo
  • South of France
  • Southerner
  • sign in (verb)  | sign in to
  • thank-yous
  • traveler | traveling | travel
  • until | ‘til (not “till”)
  • volcanoes

  • catching some rays
  • crazy
  • cuisine
  • exotic
  • extraordinary
  • fellow traveler
  • foodie
  • fun in the sun
  • galore
  • globe-trotting
  • gorgeous
  • guru
  • insane
  • it’s the perfect time / it’s the perfect place
  • jet-setting
  • junkie (as in adrenaline junkie, etc.)
  • leaf-peeping
  • Mother Nature (just say “nature”)
  • multicultural
  • paradise
  • something for everyone
  • to wit
  • unique
  • wanderlust
  • Zen-like
  • Zen
