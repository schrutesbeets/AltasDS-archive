Buttons

Buttons trigger an action such as submitting a form or showing/hiding an interface component.

Best practices

 

Hierarchy

Do

Reserve the Brand Button for the highest priority action on a page. Use Primary Buttons for page-level actions that move the user forward in a flow. Secondary and Borderless buttons can be used for all other section-level actions.

Don't

Do not ignore button hierarchy. Overuse of the Brand Button diminishes its prominence on the page. Interchanging Primary, Secondary and Borderless Buttons reduces usersâ€™ understanding of how to move forward in the experience.

Do

Only use the Ollie AI variant for entering the Ask Ollie AI travel companion experience, and for actions within that experience.

Don't

Do not use the Ollie AI variant outside of the Ask Ollie AI travel companion experience context. Do not modify other Buttons to use Trip AI tokens.

 

Usage

Do

Use buttons for discrete actions. For multiple actions, choose a higher-emphasis button for the more important action such as: Primary with Secondary Button, or Primary with Borderless Button.
