Michelin Label ðŸ¥¶

The Michelin Label is used on cards and shelves (on list pages in particular) to highlight Michelin awarded restaurants.

  • Overview
  • Dos & Don'ts

Best practices

 

Usage

Do

Append the Michelin Label to Restaurants that have won the award.

Don't

Do not append the Michelin Label to Restaurants or other travel products that have not won the award.

Do

Use the Three-Star variant to represent "Exceptional cuisine, worth a special journey!"

Do

Use the Two-Star variant to represent "Excellent cooking, worth a detour!"

Do

Use the One-Star variant to represent "High quality cooking, worth a stop!"

Do

Use the Plate variant to represent "Good cooking."

Do

Use the Bib Gourmand variant to represent "Good quality, good value cooking."

 

Appearance

Do

Use the respective Michelin color tokens applied to the container () and text ().

Don't

Do not change the color tokens applied to the Michelin Label.

Do

Use the Michelin Label in its supported size and shape.

Don't

Do not change the size or shape of the Michelin Label.

 

Content

Do

Retain the â€œMichelinâ€ label text in all caps.

Don't

Do not modify the Michelin Label text in any way.

Do

Retain the supported Michelin Stars used for the Michelin Label.

Do

Do not modify or change the stars used for the Michelin Label.
